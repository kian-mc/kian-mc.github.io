The e-bike (01/2020 - 08/2020)

About a year ago my dad and I started working on and designing an electric bicycle. The frame/base of the bike is one we got for free because someone was giving it away. The motor is a 24v brushed DC motor, we used a battery we got from an old scooter that was 37v. The brains of the bike is a 36v ESC we got on Amazon along with the throttle from the scooter. At a full charge, it can go up to 20 MPH, which classifies it as a class 2 e-bike.

e-Bike Repair (10/2020)

For a while now, I have been doing some freelance work for a local bike shop. Usually, I help them with electronics stuff like soldering wires or diagnosing and repairing electric bicycles that customers bring in. This was my first repair job and it went well because of the simplicity of the problem. The bike had pedal-assist and the sensor was not correctly orientated on the crankshaft, therefore not letting any power go to the motor.

On-Air LED Project (10/2020)

Because of all the bad stuff happening in 2020, most people have been forced to work from home. In my home, one of the people living there has had to use Zoom for meetings and because of the place where she uses Zoom, we sometimes walk in on a meeting. This is a problem because it interrupts the meeting and it makes her more stressed and angry. That is why I made an LED system where there are three modes, green, red, and off. If she is in the meeting she can turn the light to red to indicate that she is in a meeting and when the meeting is over she can use green or off.
